## Completed Quests

### Q-001 · The Evening Patrol
**Date:** June 25, 2026 · **Type:** Recovery Scout · **Status:** ✅ Completed

Halbarad ordered a standard evening perimeter circuit — three leagues around Camp Harnost — before the Company entered its final preparations for the march south. Limrandir set off alone into the cooling dusk, the Arnorian roads familiar underfoot. The circuit turned up nothing alarming: overgrown track-ways, a shepherd's collapsed bothy, the distant ruin of an Arthedain marker-stone. But as he rounded the northern approach, wolf-howls drifted in from the Weather Hills — sharp, communicative howls, not the random calls of a lonely pack. He logged the bearing and returned.

Halbarad listened to his report and said, "Note the direction every night. Don't raise it with the others." Limrandir understood: whatever was in the Weather Hills, Halbarad already knew it was there.

*Training Evidence: 3.3 km, ~21 min, recovery effort, avg HR 128 — East Hartford Running, July 9 (mapped to June 25 patrol).*

### Q-002 · The Trial of Messenger-Speed
**Date:** June 28, 2026 · **Type:** Race — Selection Trial · **Status:** ✅ Completed — 2nd Place

Halbarad called every Ranger seeking the role of Swift Messenger to a five-league race around the eastern circuit of Camp Harnost. The winner would carry the Company's most urgent dispatches ahead of the main body. The stakes were plain — this was not honor alone but a role that would keep its holder running every day of the march south.

Limrandir ran hard from the first step, setting his pace by the granite outcrops he had mapped the week before. He passed four men on the second league and held his place through the third. But Golasgil could not be caught. The man ran with an eerie ease — never seeming to labor for breath, never hitching at the hills — and crossed the finish two minutes ahead of Limrandir's second-place time. Halbarad awarded Golasgil the role of Swift Messenger and gave Limrandir the title of Scout of the Advance: a role requiring speed, yes, but also precision of observation and cartographic skill that Golasgil plainly did not have.

Both men shook hands. The apparent friendship felt genuine. Limrandir told himself that.

*Training Evidence: 5.0 km, 19:50 min, race effort — avg HR 175 — GRR Firecracker 5K, 2nd place, June 28.*

### Q-003 · The Ten-League Survey
**Date:** June 30, 2026 · **Type:** Long Scouting Run — Mapping · **Status:** ✅ Completed

Halbarad tasked Limrandir with a full survey of the first ten leagues south through Cardolan — ford conditions, campsite viability, mile-stone conditions, and signs of enemy presence. He ran the length of it before midday and returned by evening with detailed charts.

The maps were good. The news was worse. At an old Arnorian way-station three leagues south of camp, Limrandir found a cold firepit and the unmistakeable boot-sole imprints of orc-scouts in the soft earth — large, splayed, clawed at the toe. No more than two weeks old, likely less. The Enemy had scouted this very road recently. Halbarad changed the Company's planned route without explanation to the others, simply telling them the Scout of the Advance had found better ground. He did not tell them what else Limrandir had found.

*Training Evidence: 9.97 km, ~54 min, avg HR 153, aerobic base, 70m elevation gain — June 30.*

### Q-004 · The Western Perimeter
**Date:** July 2, 2026 · **Type:** Base Patrol · **Status:** ✅ Completed

A six-league sweep of the western flank — rougher going than the south road. Collapsed farmsteads, old field-walls swallowed by bracken, the ghost of a mill-race long dry. Limrandir completed the circuit and returned with a question he phrased carefully to Halbarad: had any Ranger been sent on an independent western patrol two days prior?

No, Halbarad said. None.

Then someone had been on that ground who was not Company. Near the stream-crossing on the second league, Limrandir had found boot-prints in the mud: Ranger-made boots — the sole-pattern used by every man in the Company — but the gait wrong. Not a patrol stride. The stride of a man walking to meet someone, then returning by the same route. Halbarad listened to every detail and then said, with absolute calm: "Say nothing to the others." The prints were measured against every Ranger's common boot. Four men wore that size. Golasgil was one of them.

*Training Evidence: 6.1 km, ~33 min, avg HR 149, 38m elevation gain — July 2.*

### Q-005 · The Great Muster Run
**Date:** July 5, 2026 · **Type:** Long Run — Major Scout · **Status:** ✅ Completed — Legendary Effort

On the eve of the Company's departure, Halbarad asked Limrandir for the impossible: run the full twenty-league advance of the first day's march through northern Cardolan, map it completely, and return before the Company set out in the morning. The request was made quietly and without fanfare, but every Ranger near the fire heard it and went still.

Limrandir left before noon. He ran through the afternoon heat, through the long fading gold of the evening, through the first hour of dusk. He crossed two fords, traversed one earthwork so old it predated Arnor itself, and skirted two barrow-hollows where the air felt thick and wrong. He returned to camp well after the stars had appeared, hollow-eyed and walking with the stiff economy of a man who has pushed his body to its exact limit and not past it. He spread his charts on the ground and began explaining.

Halbarad studied them for a long time. He said: "You've saved us two days." Elladan, standing behind him, offered only a nod. But Elrohir caught Limrandir's eye from across the fire and gave a slow, deliberate nod of his own — the nod of a man who has decided something.

*Training Evidence: 19.99 km, ~108 min, avg HR 153, aerobic base, 136m elevation gain — July 5.*

### Q-006 · First League into Cardolan
**Date:** July 7, 2026 · **Type:** Base Scout · **Status:** ✅ Completed

The Company now moving, Limrandir ran six leagues ahead of the main body into the fringe-lands of Cardolan. The country changes here. The air carries a heaviness — not weather, not fatigue — the particular weight of old grief made permanent in the landscape. Mounds rise in clusters from the flat ground. Roads that were once wide and good are sinking back into the earth. The mile-stones of Arnor stand at wrong angles, as if the ground beneath them shifted long ago and never stopped.

He found two suitable campsites on elevated ground well away from the nearest barrows, marked three stretches where the road ran too close to wight-hollow ground, and returned before dusk with a detailed chart. Halbarad implemented his recommendations without revision. Elladan reviewed the barrow-avoidance markings and added his own notations in Tengwar at two points. He did not explain what they meant, but he left the map on Limrandir's gear with them visible.

*Training Evidence: 6.13 km, ~31 min, avg HR 144, aerobic base, 57m elevation gain — July 7.*

### Q-007 · The Pursuit Test
**Date:** July 8, 2026 · **Type:** Threshold Run — Training Trial · **Status:** ✅ Completed

Halbarad's test was simple in design and brutal in execution: Limrandir would run as quarry while three of the Company's best trackers attempted to shadow him undetected over six leagues of broken ground. If they could mark his position within a hundred paces after the run, he was too predictable for advance scouting.

Limrandir ran in surging intervals — easing back to draw a shadow close, then throwing in a hard burst to create distance, then cutting sideways off the road entirely, through a drainage ditch filled with nettles, up a rocky scramble that left no footprint, and doubling back under the ridgeline. He lost the first shadow on the second league, the second on the third. The last — Halbador, the Company's most experienced tracker — pursued him to a hilltop where Limrandir had simply stopped and lain flat in the bracken for ten minutes. Halbador passed within thirty feet and never looked down. Test passed. Halbarad declared it without ceremony, which was ceremony enough.

*Training Evidence: 5.55 km, ~28 min, avg HR 143, threshold/tempo with intervals, max HR 167 — July 8.*

### Q-008 · The Watch-tower Retrieval
**Date:** July 9, 2026 · **Type:** Climbing & Recovery Scout · **Status:** ✅ Completed

Word arrived that a Ranger courier had secured dispatches in the crook of the stairwell at the ruined Arnorian watch-tower two leagues east — and the courier had not returned to camp. The tower's outer stairway had collapsed long ago. The only way up was the interior face: loose stone, crumbling mortar, a thirty-foot climb with no purchase to trust.

Limrandir climbed it. The satchel was wedged in a Ranger-cleft near the top, exactly where courier-doctrine placed it. Inside: a letter from one of Aragorn's southern contacts, written in a careful cipher he decoded on the walk back. The message confirmed what the howls in the Weather Hills had suggested — a Nazgul had been sighted moving westward past the Misty Mountains. One of the Nine was no longer simply guarding the Black Land. It was ranging. He delivered the news to Halbarad and ran a short recovery circuit of the south road while Halbarad and Elladan conferred privately. The camp that night was quieter than usual.

*Training Evidence: Indoor climbing — 106m total ascent, 16 routes, 35 min active / 3.3 km recovery run — July 9.*

Limrandir's assigned patrol of the southern Cardolan road was volunteered away by Golasgil, who claimed the Scout of the Advance needed rest after the tower climb. It was a plausible offer. Limrandir accepted.

That evening, Golasgil returned and reported the road clear. At dusk, Elladan rode out to verify — and found fresh warg-prints on the very stretch Golasgil claimed to have surveyed. A scout pack. Heavy, four-toed, moving south. When Halbarad called Golasgil to give his account, the Ranger's description of the route had gaps: landmarks named that did not exist, a ford described that lay on a different road entirely. ## Active Quests

### Q-010 · The Long Road of Cardolan
**Date:** July 11, 2026 · **Type:** Long Run — Major Scout · **Status:** ⬜ Open

> *"The barrow-downs are vast and the Company cannot ride blind through them. Something is moving there — not wight, not orc, not anything I have a name for yet. I need Limrandir's eyes on that ground before we commit to the southern track."* — Elladan, to Halbarad

The Company has reached the deep heart of Cardolan. Limrandir must run a long advance scout into the most treacherous section of the Barrow Downs — far beyond the Company's current camp — to find and verify a safe passage through to the southern side. The wights are drawn to hollow ground, and the road ahead dips through exactly the kind of geography they favor. He must map the true safe line, mark the camp position on the far side, and return before dark.

A long, steady effort is required — this is not a sprint but an endurance task. The distance is significant. His body will know it by the end.

**Consequence if skipped:** The Company marches blind into the Barrow Downs. Night falls before they clear the worst of the hollow ground. The wights rise. In the confusion, Maedhron — the youngest Ranger — is drawn off the road and into the barrows. By the time the Company hears him call out, he is beyond reach. He is not recovered.

*Workout: Long Run · Steady Aerobic Effort · July 11, 2026*

### Q-011 · Shadows at the Greenway
**Date:** July 12, 2026 · **Type:** Base Run — Perimeter Scout · **Status:** ⬜ Open

> *"The Greenway has not been safe for a generation. But right now it is watched — and whoever is watching it watched us approach. I want to know if they have gone south already or if they are waiting."* — Halbarad

A reliable road-warden contact left word that a solitary figure was observed moving along the Greenway at irregular hours — never the same pattern twice, never the same section two nights running. Could be a refugee. Could be a warg-scout in disguise. Could be the same watcher Elladan spotted near the Barrow Downs. Limrandir is tasked with a base-effort circuit of the Greenway crossing and its approaches — running steady and quiet, not fast. Speed would betray his presence. This is observation work.

**Consequence if skipped:** The unidentified watcher observes the Company's Greenway crossing from cover. By the next morning, a rider departs south at speed. Where the rider is bound remains unknown — but Halbarad believes: Orthanc or beyond.

*Workout: Base Run · Aerobic Effort · July 12, 2026*

### Q-012 · Sprint to Halbador
**Date:** July 13, 2026 · **Type:** Anaerobic Run — Urgent Intercept · **Status:** ⬜ Open

> *"Halbador left at dawn with Maedhron and two others. The south fork. Limrandir — your chart from last night. Show me."* — Halbarad
> *"...That road has fresh orc-sign. Two days old, maybe less."*
> *"Then run."*

Halbador led a small advance party down the south fork road at first light — a road Limrandir's most recent charts flag as newly compromised by an orc scouting party. The Company cannot send a rider without alerting any watcher on the high ground. Limrandir must run — hard and without pause — to intercept Halbador's party before they reach the ambush point at the river-bend. The gap is closing. He has a window of perhaps forty minutes.

This is not a patrol. This is a sprint from start to finish.

**Consequence if skipped:** Halbador's party walks into the orc position at the river-bend. In the ambush, two Rangers are killed and Maedhron is wounded. The Company is delayed by a full day while they regroup, and Halbarad privately holds the delayed warning — not the ambush itself — as a failure of the advance scout function.

*Workout: Anaerobic Run · High Intensity Intervals · July 13, 2026*

### Q-013 · The Ferryman's Intelligence
**Date:** July 15, 2026 · **Type:** Base Run — Intelligence Gathering · **Status:** ⬜ Open

> *"There is a man at the Tharbad quay who has been there longer than anyone remembers. He was a Gondorian scout once. He talks for wine and talks well. Elladan met him, years ago, when this land still had roads worth walking. Go to him before the Company arrives. He will not speak to a group."* — Elrohir

Limrandir runs ahead to the broken quays of old Tharbad to find Dirwaith — ancient ferryman, former Gondorian field scout, keeper of the southern roads' long memory. Dirwaith knows what has moved through the Gap of Rohan in recent months and he knows the Greyflood crossing stone by stone. He will speak to a single Ranger. He will not speak to thirty. A steady base pace — this is not a race. He needs to arrive composed and ready to listen.

**Consequence if skipped:** The Company reaches Tharbad without Dirwaith's intelligence on the ford or the Gap. When they attempt the crossing at the most obvious point, the current proves treacherous. A horse and its rider are swept downstream. The rider survives, but the saddlebag — carrying Limrandir's provisional charts of the next week's route — does not.

*Workout: Base Run · Aerobic Effort · July 15, 2026*

### Q-014 · The Ford Before Dark
**Date:** July 16, 2026 · **Type:** Base Run — Pre-Crossing Survey · **Status:** ⬜ Open

> *"I would rather have a Ranger run every stone of that ford than trust it unseen. The Greyflood is not merciful to the confident."* — Halbarad

The ancient bridge at Tharbad fell long ago. The ford is passable — but only in the right line, at the right hour, with the right knowledge of where the bottom drops away into nothing. Dirwaith will tell Limrandir the line tomorrow morning. Tonight, Limrandir runs the river bank in both directions, mapping the ford from both sides, marking depth-changes with stakes, and confirming the Company can begin crossing at first light. The work is methodical and requires a steady effort across multiple miles of uneven river-bank ground.

**Consequence if skipped:** The Company attempts the crossing without a surveyed line. Three horses stumble into the deep channel. Two are lamed. A third carries Elladan's gear, which is lost to the current. The Company reaches the south bank cold, ragged, and half a day behind schedule — and behind schedule, in this march, means everything.

*Workout: Base Run · Aerobic Effort · July 16, 2026*
