### Q-010 · The Long Road of Cardolan ✅ COMPLETED

**Date:** July 11 (completed July 12), 2026 · **Type:** Long Run — Major Scout

Limrandir ran thirteen leagues deep into the Barrow Downs — the longest and most perilous scout of the campaign. He passed through country so heavy with old grief that even the light seemed dim. At the third league, he saw something watching from a standing stone: not a wight, but something darker. It did not follow.

He marked the safe passage through the hollow ground, verified two elevated camp positions, and returned with complete charts. Halbarad implemented every recommendation. That night, from the elevated camp, the Company watched tall shapes moving among the distant barrows. They did not approach. Whatever they were, they had let Limrandir pass.
